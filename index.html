Our Story

Transforming snacking experience with traditional flavors and quality ingredients.

Based out of Howrah, near to the heart of city of joy, Kolkata, we are committed to bring the traditional flavors at it's best quality in variety of Indian snacks that make snacking a fun. From crispy namkeens to dietary snacks, every bite is crafted with traditional recipes, premium ingredients, and a whole lot of love. For us, it’s not just about taste—it’s about keeping the essence of our culture alive in every packet.
